# Code of Conduct

Nexus Engine adopts the **Contributor Covenant v2.1** as its code of conduct.

Full text: <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

## Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level. We commit to acting in ways that contribute to an open, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Scope

This Code applies to all project spaces — repositories, issue trackers, pull requests, discussions, chat channels, events — and when an individual is representing the project in public spaces.

## Expected behavior

- Demonstrate empathy and kindness toward other people.
- Be respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes, apologize, and learn from them.
- Focus on what is best for the overall community.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment of any kind.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Sexualized language, imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

See the full Covenant text (linked above) for the complete enumeration and the enforcement-ladder definitions (Correction, Warning, Temporary Ban, Permanent Ban).

## Enforcement

Report violations to `conduct@developerz.ai` [TBD]. All reports are reviewed promptly and confidentially. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and safety of the reporter.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by other project leadership.
